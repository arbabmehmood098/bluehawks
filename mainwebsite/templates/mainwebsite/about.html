WHO WE ARE

FSS

FSS is one of the pioneers in the security business of Pakistan. Founded in 1993, with the noble aim of providing employment opportunities to the retired Army personnel and generate funds for the families of Shuhadas, Army widows and welfare of soldiers. The Company earned a name in the country for providing well-trained and reliable security services to the national/multinational public and private sector organizations. With 32 years of its operational experience in Pakistan's most difficult law and order period, FSS has matured and diversified its services to meet the needs of even the most discerning clients.
